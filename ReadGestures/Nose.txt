Pinching bridge of nose: Gesture usually accompanied with closed eyes communicates great thought and concern about the decision to be made. A person in self-conflict might lower his head and pinch the bridge of his nose to test if the situation is reality. Wait for person to express his doubts.
Touching or slightly rubbing nose, usually with index: Shows negative opinion/ contrary thoughts. Doubt sign. Touch is very light, not an itch. Can also be behind ear, or rubbing the eye.
